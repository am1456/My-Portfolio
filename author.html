My Journey to Writing

I come from a Bengali family where we have a huge influence of extracurricular activities like singing, dancing, and recitation. My family has people who are pursuing singing as their career and teaching profession. Hence, I was also drawn into singing, and in Bengali singing, we have a totally different journey which belongs to Kobi Guru Rabindranath Tagore, called Rabindra Sangeet. Those songs are still my favorites, which introduced me to Rabindranath Tagore and later Kazi Nazrul Islam.

Later, when I got introduced to other Bengali literature in my school days, I discovered other great writers like Bankim Chandra Chattopadhyay, Sarat Chandra Chattopadhyay, and Bibhutibhushan Bandopadhyay and many other talented writters. The story that influenced me most is Pather Panchali (my favorite novel, and I remember each line of that story). Till that time, my reach in literature is only where my school taught us‚Äînot a single point above it.

Then I passed Class 10, and Class 11 turned out to be the most depressing year of my life. Even after studying intensely, I couldn't score much. I am an average student, not a topper, but I usually used to get 85%, but in Class 11, I got 65%+. As a result, I fell into depression.

To cope with this depression, I started watching animation, more specifically Anime and Donghua. It just opened a fascinating world in front of me. Then I started reading Manga and Manhua, and then Light Novels, and then Web Novels written by the anime's original authors. This introduced me to worlds linked to the world of animation. And here I am now, writing a web novel myself.

"From Bengali literature to anime, from depression to creativity‚Äîevery experience shaped my journey as a storyteller."

When I came to college, I got some free time in my first year to think about a story. Along with that, I met a college mate of mine with whom I discussed a basic story structure‚Äîwhat could happen in the story and how to progress it. (By the way, this is not the present version because I underwent many modifications.)

Finally, when I came back home during my summer break, a rough sketch of the story was inside my mind. But still, I did many hit and trials. I used to write even five chapters but couldn't progress beyond it. Hence, I needed to scrap it and start from the beginning. And now I am where I am.

Finally, in the month of August 2024, I posted my story on Royal Road. Things are growing pretty good for me, at least.
